# SOCIAL MEDIA DO’S & DON’TS
## DO's:
- Protect your personal information (use privacy settings)
- Maintain a professional and respectful tone
- Think before posting anything online
- Share useful, positive, and meaningful content
- Verify information before sharing (avoid fake news)
## DON'Ts:
- Do not engage in arguments or online trolling
- Do not share sensitive personal details publicly
- Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
- Do not harm your digital reputation with careless posts
- Avoid spreading unverified or false information
# • Be Smart • Be Safe • Be Responsible •
